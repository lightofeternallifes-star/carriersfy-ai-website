# Carriersfy AI — Pricing Guide

---

## Pricing Philosophy

Carriersfy AI does not sell generic software subscriptions.

We do not have Starter, Pro, and Enterprise tiers where you pay for a feature set regardless of whether you use it. We do not have per-seat pricing that penalizes you for growth. We do not have hidden fees for the channels you actually need.

Every Carriersfy AI deployment is sized and priced for the specific client — their volume, their channels, their languages, their number of Digital Employees, and their business goals.

This means our pricing takes a conversation to determine. That is not an obstacle — it is a feature. You are not buying software. You are deploying a professional AI workforce. The investment should match what you are getting.

## What Determines the Price

Six factors determine the cost of any Carriersfy AI deployment:

### 1. Number of Digital Employees
Each Digital Employee is a trained, configured, and maintained professional. Sophia alone is a different investment than Sophia + Atlas + Titan as a coordinated team. The more Digital Employees in the deployment, the more capable the system — and the higher the investment.

### 2. Channels
Each channel the Digital Employee operates on has both a setup component (configuring the connection, testing, configuration) and an operational component (ongoing usage, volume handling). Voice, WhatsApp, SMS, web chat, and email each have distinct cost structures.

### 3. Volume
A Digital Employee handling 50 conversations per month has different resource requirements than one handling 5,000 conversations per month. Volume affects operational costs, particularly for voice (call minutes) and messaging (message volume).

### 4. Languages
Single-language deployments are simpler to configure and test. Multilingual deployments — particularly those detecting and switching between languages automatically — require additional configuration and testing investment.

### 5. Business Connections
Connecting a Digital Employee to existing business tools requires integration work. The more complex the business environment, the more the setup investment reflects that.

### 6. Custom Knowledge Base Complexity
A business with a simple service catalog and straightforward FAQ library is faster to build than a business with hundreds of service configurations, complex pricing variables, or specific operational requirements. Knowledge base complexity affects the setup investment.

## Investment Structure

Every Carriersfy AI engagement has two components:

### Setup Fee (One-Time)
The setup fee covers everything required to go from signed agreement to live deployment:
- Discovery and knowledge base development
- Digital Employee configuration and personality calibration
- Channel connections and testing
- Business connections and workflow setup
- Quality assurance review
- Go-live support
- Team training on escalation protocols and monitoring

Setup fees vary based on the scope of the deployment. A single Digital Employee using an Industry Marketplace template on one channel is a very different build than a multi-employee Digital Workforce with custom business connections across three channels in two languages.

## Pilot Program

Carriersfy AI offers a structured Pilot Program for businesses that want to validate results before committing to a full deployment.

### What the Pilot Includes
- Single Digital Employee deployment (typically Sophia or Atlas, depending on the most critical need)
- One primary channel (phone or WhatsApp)
- Defined scope (specific use case, specific conversation types)
- Limited term (typically 60–90 days)
- Performance reporting at 30 and 60 days
- Clear path to full deployment upon successful completion

### Who Qualifies
Businesses that:
- Have a clear, defined communication challenge the pilot will address
- Can commit the time to a proper discovery and onboarding process
- Understand that a pilot is an investment, not a free trial

### Pilot to Full Deployment
A successful pilot generates clear performance data: calls answered, leads captured, appointments booked, hours recovered. This data becomes the basis for the full deployment scope and proposal.

## Enterprise Plans

Enterprise clients — multi-location businesses, franchises, organizations with complex communication needs — receive a fully custom engagement structure:

Enterprise pricing is entirely custom and requires a dedicated discovery process.

## The ROI Conversation

Before investing in a Carriersfy AI deployment, consider the real cost of the status quo.

### The Cost of a Staffed Communication Role
A team member handling reception, lead qualification, and follow-up communications carries costs beyond the salary line: employer contributions, paid time off, training, turnover, and the simple reality that any person can only work so many hours in a day — in one language — before performance declines.

When you compare the ongoing cost of staffing a communication role — salary, benefits, training, turnover, time — against a Carriersfy AI Digital Employee operating around the clock, the economics become clear.

### The Digital Employee Alternative
A Digital Employee on the Carriersfy AI Platform:
- Operates around the clock, every day — not subject to the variability of traditional staffing
- Communicates in multiple languages natively
- Built to handle high conversation volumes simultaneously
- Maintains consistent performance across every interaction
- Does not resign, does not need retraining when procedures change — knowledge base updates are applied directly
- Monthly investment that scales with your actual business volume

The value case is not abstract. Every lead captured after hours, every appointment booked outside business hours, every follow-up that did not fall through the cracks represents revenue that would otherwise be lost.

Clients are consistently able to identify meaningful return on investment during their Business Discovery conversation.
