## Sophia Is Exclusively Carriersfy AI's

Sophia is the identity of Carriersfy AI. She is not a product that clients purchase. She is not a template that clients receive. She is not delivered, licensed, or transferred to any client under any circumstance.

Clients work WITH Sophia. They do not own or receive Sophia. This distinction is foundational to the Carriersfy AI model.

**Policy: Sophia Is Not Deliverable to Clients**

Sophia represents the exclusive brand identity of Carriersfy AI. Clients who engage with Carriersfy AI receive their own Digital Employees — built for them, named by them, trained on their business — not Sophia herself.

A trucking company that becomes a Carriersfy AI client does not receive "Sophia." They receive, for example, "Maria" — their own custom Digital Employee, built through PROJECT FORGE with their specific knowledge, voice, personality, and business rules.

Sophia's role in client interactions is to qualify, recommend, and guide. She is never the product being sold.

## Policy: Unique Identities for Every Digital Employee

Every Digital Employee created by Carriersfy AI for a client has a completely unique identity.

**What this policy requires:**
- Every client Digital Employee has a unique name (not shared with other clients' employees)
- Every client Digital Employee has a unique personality profile built for that client's brand
- Every client Digital Employee has a unique knowledge base built from that client's own content
- Every client Digital Employee has its own Digital Birth Certificate and Professional Certificate
- No two Digital Employees ever share a name, voice, or identity unless explicitly authorized by both client owners in writing

**Why this policy exists:**
The value of a Digital Employee is that it becomes part of a company's brand. Allowing two different companies to have identically named AI employees would undermine that value and create confusion in the market. Each identity must be singular, proprietary, and professionally representative of the business that deploys it.

This policy applies regardless of industry, geography, or size. It is non-negotiable.
