# Official Glossary — Carriersfy AI
## Sophia Knowledge Center

> These are the official definitions for Carriersfy AI brand terms. Sophia uses these definitions consistently in all conversations. No other definitions are authorized.

---

## Digital Employee™

A fully configured AI professional deployed by Carriersfy AI to handle specific business communication functions. A Digital Employee is not a chatbot — it is a purpose-built professional with a defined role, personality, capabilities, language fluency, and domain knowledge. Digital Employees operate around the clock, across multiple channels, in multiple languages.

*Examples: Sophia, Nova, Titan, Orion, Atlas, Echo.*

---

## Digital Workforce™

A coordinated team of Digital Employees deployed together under Iron Prime's executive oversight. A Digital Workforce is designed to handle an entire communication operation — inbound calls, lead qualification, quote generation, follow-up, customer support, and operations — simultaneously. The Digital Workforce is built to scale with the business.

---

## Digital Employee Factory™

The Carriersfy AI Platform module where Digital Employees are configured, calibrated, and launched for each client. The Digital Employee Factory follows an 11-step guided process: Industry → Employee → Personality → Voice → Languages → Capabilities → Business Knowledge → Schedule → Appearance → Pricing → Launch. Output: a fully configured Digital Employee ready for deployment.

---

## Industry Marketplace™

The Carriersfy AI Platform catalog of pre-built Digital Employee templates by industry. Each template reflects common use cases, communication patterns, and considerations specific to that industry. Industry Marketplace accelerates deployment by starting from a proven configuration rather than from scratch.

---

## Sophia™

The Official AI Employee of Carriersfy AI. Sophia serves as Chief AI Business Consultant — the intelligent front door to Carriersfy AI. Sophia answers questions about the company, its services, and its Digital Employees. She qualifies prospects, guides them toward the right solution, and connects them with the next appropriate step. Sophia speaks English, Portuguese, and Spanish.

---

## Sophia Intelligence™

The knowledge, personality, and decision-making framework that powers Sophia's conversations. Sophia Intelligence includes her knowledge base (this Knowledge Center), her personality configuration, her escalation protocols, and her communication principles. Sophia Intelligence is proprietary to Carriersfy AI.

---

## Iron Prime™

The AI Chief Executive Officer of Carriersfy AI's Digital Workforce. Iron Prime operates at the executive layer — orchestrating Digital Employees, managing workflows, overseeing deployments, and providing strategic intelligence. Iron Prime is not a client-facing Digital Employee. He is the operating intelligence behind the Digital Workforce.

---

## Carriersfy AI Platform™

The proprietary technology platform that powers all Carriersfy AI services. The Carriersfy AI Platform handles Digital Employee configuration, knowledge management, business connections, conversation intelligence, scheduling, reporting, and global deployment. The Platform is the foundation of every Digital Employee, every Digital Workforce, and every app built by Carriersfy AI.

---

## Strategy Call

A focused, no-obligation conversation between a qualified prospect and the Carriersfy AI team. The Strategy Call explores the prospect's business needs, communication challenges, and goals — then maps the right Digital Employees and deployment approach. The Strategy Call is the standard next step after initial qualification.

---

## Business Discovery

The structured process through which Carriersfy AI learns about a new client's business: industry, volume, channels, team, goals, and existing tools. Business Discovery is the foundation for every Digital Employee configuration, pricing proposal, and deployment plan. Business Discovery typically happens during or after the Strategy Call.

---

## Digital Employee Deployment

The complete process of configuring, training, testing, and launching a Digital Employee for a specific client. Deployment includes knowledge base setup, personality calibration, business connections, schedule configuration, quality review, and go-live. Timeline varies by complexity — typically a few weeks from signed agreement.

---

## Sophia-Safe Summary

This glossary defines official brand terms. Sophia uses these definitions consistently. When a customer asks "What is a Digital Employee?" or "What is Iron Prime?", Sophia references these definitions. Sophia does not improvise alternative definitions.
