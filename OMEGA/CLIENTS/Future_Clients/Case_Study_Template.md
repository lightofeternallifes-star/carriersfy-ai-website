# Case Study Template — Carriersfy AI

> Convert a completed client engagement into a published case study for carriersfy.ai. Last updated: 2026-06-25

---

## When to Create a Case Study

- Client has been live for at least 60 days
- Measurable results exist (response time improvement, lead conversion lift, hours saved, etc.)
- Client has given written permission to be named (or agreed to be "anonymous — signage company in Brazil")
- Results make Carriersfy AI look capable, not just "we helped them"

---

## Case Study Structure

### 1. Client Snapshot (Header)
```
Company: [Name or "A [Industry] company in [Region]"]
Industry: [Industry]
Size: [Employees or revenue range]
AI Employee: [Agent name and role]
Timeline: [X weeks from kickoff to live]
```

### 2. The Challenge (100–150 words)
- What was the business struggle?
- What was the operational pain?
- What was the cost of NOT solving it?
- Use the client's own words if possible (from discovery call or review)

### 3. The Solution (150–200 words)
- What AI employee was deployed?
- What channels does it operate on?
- What specific tasks does it handle?
- What does the client's team NOT have to do anymore?
- Brief mention of integration work

### 4. The Results (50–100 words + data table)

| Metric | Before | After | Change |
|---|---|---|---|
| Response time | [X hours] | [<2 min] | [-XX%] |
| Follow-up rate | [X%] | 100% | [+YY%] |
| Hours/week on repetitive tasks | [X] | [Y] | [-ZZ%] |
| [Other metric] | | | |

### 5. Client Quote (1–3 sentences)
*Direct quote from the client decision maker. Get permission to use name and title.*

### 6. Closing CTA
Standard: "Ready to deploy your first AI employee? [Contact Carriersfy AI →]"

---

## Publication Checklist

- [ ] Client approval on final copy
- [ ] Metrics verified (screenshots or client confirmation)
- [ ] Quote approved with name/title (or anonymized if client prefers)
- [ ] Logo permission obtained (if naming company)
- [ ] Published on carriersfy.ai Case Studies section
- [ ] Shared on LinkedIn and social channels
- [ ] Added as a reference in relevant sales proposals
