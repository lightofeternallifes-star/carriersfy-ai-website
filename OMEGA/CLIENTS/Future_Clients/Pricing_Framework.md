# Pricing Framework — Carriersfy AI

> How to price AI employee deployments. Last updated: 2026-06-25

---

## Pricing Philosophy

Price against the cost of a human hire, not against software tools.

A customer service or operations employee in the U.S. costs:
- **Salary:** $35,000–$65,000/year ($2,900–$5,400/month)
- **Benefits + payroll taxes:** ~30% overhead → $3,700–$7,000/month fully loaded
- **Training time:** 2–4 weeks before productive
- **Turnover cost:** 6–9 months salary to replace

A Carriersfy AI employee:
- Deploys in 3–4 weeks
- Costs $1,000–$5,000/month (all-in)
- Works 24/7
- Never calls in sick
- Improves over time
- No turnover

**Frame every proposal around this comparison.**

---

## Standard Pricing Tiers

### Tier 1 — Starter ($2,500 setup + $1,000/month)
- 1 AI employee
- 1 primary channel (email or WhatsApp or web chat)
- Up to 3 conversation flows
- Basic CRM integration (read-only or simple write)
- Monthly performance report
- Best for: Solo operators or small businesses with a single, well-defined use case

### Tier 2 — Growth ($5,000 setup + $2,000/month)
- 1 AI employee, fully trained
- Up to 3 channels
- Up to 8 conversation flows
- Full CRM integration (read + write)
- Biweekly performance review
- 1 monthly optimization session
- Best for: SMBs with multi-channel customer communication

### Tier 3 — Enterprise ($10,000+ setup + $3,500+/month)
- 2+ AI employees
- Unlimited channels
- Custom workflow automation
- ERP/CRM/calendar full integration
- Weekly performance reviews
- Dedicated support
- Best for: Businesses with complex operations and multiple departments

## Custom Pricing Inputs

When a client doesn't fit a tier, price from first principles:

| Factor | Price Adjustment |
|---|---|
| Additional AI employees | +$1,000–$2,500/month per agent |
| Complex integrations (ERP, custom API) | +$2,000–$5,000 setup; +$500/month maintenance |
| Language customization (specialized vocabulary) | +$1,000–$2,000 setup |
| Rush deployment (<2 weeks) | +25% on setup fee |
| Multi-language AI employee | +$500/month |
| Dedicated instance (no shared infrastructure) | +$500/month |

## Geographic Adjustments

| Market | Adjustment |
|---|---|
| U.S. | Standard pricing above |
| Brazil | Reduce by ~20–30% (purchasing power parity) — present in BRL |
| Latin America | Reduce by ~25–35% |

---

## Discount Policy

- **Annual prepay:** 15% discount on monthly retainer
- **Multi-client referral:** 10% off next month per referred client who signs
- **Pilot/POC discount:** Not recommended — it devalues the service. Instead, offer a 30-day money-back guarantee.

---

## What NOT to Do

- Do not discount below $750/month on retainer — it's not operationally sustainable
- Do not offer "free trials" — they attract non-serious buyers
- Do not price by the hour — we sell outcomes, not time
- Do not negotiate setup fee away entirely — it funds the actual build work
