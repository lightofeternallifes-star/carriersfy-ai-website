**U.S. SMB:**
- The fastest path to a yes is showing the comparison to a human hire cost
- Decision makers are time-poor — keep pitches under 15 minutes
- Industry-specific case studies convert better than generic AI pitches

**Brazilian market:**
- WhatsApp is non-negotiable — any AI employee must work on WhatsApp
- Trust is built through relationships first, demos second
- Pricing in BRL removes friction — avoid USD prices when targeting BR
- Response to proposals is slower — follow up within 3 days, not 7
