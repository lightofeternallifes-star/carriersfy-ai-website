# Contract Template — Carriersfy AI

> Key clauses for AI service agreements. NOT legal advice — have an attorney review before use. Last updated: 2026-06-25

---

## Essential Clauses

### 1. Scope of Services
Define explicitly what the AI employee does and what it does NOT do. Every limitation should be written into the contract.

```
Example: "The AI Employee will handle first-contact customer inquiries via [channels]. 
It will not make financial commitments, process refunds, or access systems outside 
those listed in Exhibit A (Integrations)."
```

### 2. Setup Fee and Retainer
```
- One-time setup fee: $[AMOUNT] — due upon signing
- Monthly retainer: $[AMOUNT]/month — billed on the [1st/15th] of each month
- Payment terms: Net [15/30]
- Late payment: [1.5%/month] interest after [X] days
```

### 3. Term and Termination
```
- Initial term: 3 months (non-cancellable)
- After initial term: month-to-month
- Termination notice: 30 days written notice
- Upon termination: Client receives all training data and configuration documentation
```

### 4. Data Ownership
```
The client retains ownership of all data inputted into or generated by the 
AI Employee. Carriersfy AI will not use client data to train models for 
other clients without explicit written consent.
```

### 5. AI Employee Performance and Limitations
```
Client acknowledges that AI employees are probabilistic systems and may 
occasionally produce incorrect or unexpected responses. Carriersfy AI will 
maintain a quality review process and address reported issues within [48 hours].

Carriersfy AI does not guarantee specific business outcomes (revenue, conversion 
rates, etc.) — we guarantee the operation of the AI system as specified.
```

### 6. Confidentiality
```
Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality of the other party's proprietary 
information, business data, client information, and trade secrets for the duration 
of the agreement and [2 years] thereafter.
```

### 7. Liability Cap
```
Carriersfy AI's total liability under this agreement shall not exceed the total 
fees paid in the [3 months] immediately preceding the claim.
```

### 8. Integration Responsibility
```
Client is responsible for providing valid API credentials, access, and permissions 
for any third-party integrations listed in Exhibit A. Carriersfy AI is not 
responsible for downtime caused by third-party service failures.
```

### 9. Escalation and Human Oversight
```
Client acknowledges responsibility for defining escalation paths and ensuring 
appropriate human oversight of AI Employee outputs, particularly for decisions 
involving financial commitments, legal matters, or sensitive customer situations.
```

### 9a. Intellectual Property Ownership
```
All intellectual property created by Carriersfy AI under this agreement — including 
conversation flows, knowledge bases, and AI employee configurations — remains the 
property of Carriersfy AI until full payment is received for the engagement in which 
it was created. Upon receipt of full payment, Client receives a perpetual, 
non-exclusive license to use the deliverables for their stated purpose.

Client retains full ownership of all data, content, and materials provided to 
Carriersfy AI for AI training and configuration. Carriersfy AI will not use 
Client's proprietary data to build products or services for other clients.
```

### 9b. Data Privacy and Regulatory Compliance
```
Both parties agree to comply with applicable data protection laws, including:

U.S. Clients: CAN-SPAM Act, FTC AI disclosure guidelines, and any applicable 
state laws (CCPA for California residents).

Brazilian Clients: Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (LGPD). Client acknowledges 
they are the Data Controller and Carriersfy AI is the Data Processor for all 
client data processed by the AI Employee. Carriersfy AI will process data only 
as instructed by Client and will implement appropriate security measures.

Both parties agree that all AI Employees will identify themselves as AI systems 
when sincerely asked by any human, in compliance with FTC guidance.
```

### 10. Governing Law
```
This agreement shall be governed by the laws of [specify: state for U.S. clients 
(e.g., "the State of Florida") or country for international clients 
(e.g., "Brazil, with jurisdiction in São Paulo")].

IMPORTANT: Fill in the governing jurisdiction before signing. For U.S. clients: 
name a specific state. For Brazilian clients: specify Brazilian law and a city 
for jurisdiction. Do not leave this blank.
```
