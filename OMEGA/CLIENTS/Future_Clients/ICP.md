# Ideal Customer Profile — Carriersfy AI

> Last updated: 2026-06-25 | Used by: Iron Prime (outbound targeting), Juan (sales qualification)

---

## Primary ICP: U.S. Operations-Heavy SMB

| Attribute | Target |
|---|---|
| **Geography** | United States (all regions) |
| **Language** | English |
| **Company Size** | 5–200 employees |
| **Annual Revenue** | $500K – $20M |
| **Industry** | See verticals below |
| **Decision Maker** | Owner / COO / Operations Manager |
| **Tech Maturity** | Uses email, basic CRM or spreadsheets, open to AI |
| **Pain Profile** | High volume of repetitive customer communication, understaffed ops team, slow follow-up |
| **Budget** | $1,000–$5,000/month for operational improvement |

### Target Verticals (U.S.)
1. **Trades & Services** (HVAC, plumbing, electrical, landscaping) — high quote volume, poor follow-up
2. **Distribution / Wholesale** — dealer inquiries, order status, RMA
3. **Marine / Outdoor Industry** — technical product support, dealer networks
4. **Signage / Print** — quote-heavy, repeat customer model
5. **Health & Wellness Clinics** — appointment booking, follow-up, patient communication
6. **Faith Organizations / Nonprofits** — member communication, event management, volunteer coordination

## Secondary ICP: Brazilian Business Owner

| Attribute | Target |
|---|---|
| **Geography** | Brazil (all regions, São Paulo and Rio priority) |
| **Language** | Portuguese (pt-BR) |
| **Company Size** | 5–100 employees |
| **Annual Revenue** | R$500K – R$10M |
| **Decision Maker** | Owner / Director |
| **Pain Profile** | WhatsApp-heavy operations, manual follow-up, no structured CRM |
| **Budget** | R$3,000–R$15,000/month |

### Target Verticals (Brazil)
1. **Signage / Gráfica** — high volume quoting via WhatsApp
2. **Imobiliárias** — property inquiry and lead follow-up
3. **Clínicas** — appointment and patient communication
4. **E-commerce / Retail** — order tracking, customer support

---

## Disqualifiers (Do Not Pursue)

| Signal | Why |
|---|---|
| Enterprise (1,000+ employees) | Sales cycle too long; procurement process not aligned with current stage |
| No digital presence at all | Too early-stage for AI employees |
| Regulated industries (banking, healthcare with PHI) | Compliance complexity exceeds current capability |
| B2C with pure transactional model (e.g. e-commerce only) | Lower margin, high volume support — not differentiated enough |
| Looking for "free AI tools" | Price-sensitive segment, not aligned with our value model |

---

## Qualifying Questions (for Iron Prime)

1. How many customer inquiries does your team handle per week?
2. What does your follow-up process look like for new leads?
3. What's the biggest operational bottleneck you face right now?
4. Do you currently use any automation or AI tools?
5. What would it mean for your business if your response time dropped to under 2 minutes, 24/7?
