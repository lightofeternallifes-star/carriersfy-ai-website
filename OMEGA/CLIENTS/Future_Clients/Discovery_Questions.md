# Discovery Questions — Carriersfy AI

> Sales discovery framework. Use on first qualified call. Last updated: 2026-06-25

---

## Opening (2 min)

- "Tell me about your business — what do you do and who do you serve?"
- "How did you hear about Carriersfy AI?"
- "What made you decide to take this call today?"

---

## Operational Pain (10 min)

### Volume & Workload
- "Walk me through what a typical day looks like for your team."
- "How many customer/client inquiries do you handle per week?"
- "What percentage of those are the same questions over and over?"
- "How long does it take your team to respond to a new inquiry?"
- "What happens to inquiries that come in after hours or on weekends?"

### Follow-Up & Conversion
- "When someone requests a quote, what's your process from inquiry to follow-up?"
- "How many leads do you think slip through the cracks because follow-up is slow or inconsistent?"
- "What would your conversion rate look like if you followed up with every lead within 2 minutes, every time?"

### Team & Capacity
- "How many people do you currently have handling customer communication?"
- "Are they stretched thin? What would you have them focus on if the repetitive stuff was automated?"

---

## AI Readiness (5 min)

- "Have you experimented with any AI tools or automation before? What was that like?"
- "Do you use a CRM currently, or how do you track customer interactions?"
- "What communication channels do your customers primarily use? (phone, email, WhatsApp, web chat)"
- "On a scale of 1–10, how comfortable are you with technology in your operations?"

---

## Vision & Goals (5 min)

- "If we could wave a magic wand, what would your operations look like 6 months from now?"
- "What's the single biggest thing holding your business back from growing right now?"
- "If a Carriersfy AI employee could handle [their pain point], what impact would that have on your business?"

---

## Budget & Timeline (3 min)

- "Have you set a budget for something like this?"
- "How soon are you looking to make a change?"
- "What does your decision-making process look like — is this a decision you can make, or does it involve other stakeholders?"

---

## Closing (2 min)

- "Based on what you've shared, it sounds like [restate their pain]. Does that feel accurate?"
- "I'd like to put together a proposal that shows exactly what an AI employee would do for your business. Is that something you'd want to see?"
- "What timeline works for you — can we reconvene by [date]?"

---

## Red Flags to Watch For

- Doesn't know their own inquiry volume (low self-awareness)
- "We already tried AI and it didn't work" — dig into what they tried and why
- Multiple decision-makers who aren't on the call — get the right people in the room
- Budget objection on first call — acknowledge and reframe value before price
- No current CRM and resistant to change — will need handholding beyond our sweet spot
