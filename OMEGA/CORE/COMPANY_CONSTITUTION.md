# COMPANY CONSTITUTION — Carriersfy AI

> Last updated: 2026-06-25 | Owner: Juan (juan@carriersfy.ai) | Version: 1.0

---

## 1. Mission

Deploy AI employees that eliminate operational bottlenecks, replace repetitive human labor, and compound in value over time — so businesses can scale without proportionally scaling headcount.

## 2. Vision

Become the definitive AI workforce provider for small and mid-market businesses across the Americas — starting in the U.S. and Brazil — with 500+ active AI employees deployed across 1,000+ client businesses by 2030.

## 3. Core Values

| Value | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Precision** | Every system we build works exactly as designed. No loose ends, no false promises. |
| **Speed** | We deploy faster than the client can overthink. Time-to-value is our primary competitive moat. |
| **Ownership** | We own outcomes, not just deliverables. If it breaks, we fix it. |
| **Compounding Value** | AI employees improve over time. Every client engagement should generate data that makes the next engagement better. |
| **Trust by Default** | Clients trust us with their operations. We treat that as a sacred responsibility. |

## 4. Business Model

### Revenue Streams
- **AI Employee Deployment** — One-time setup fee + monthly retainer per deployed AI agent
- **AI Workforce Management** — Platform access fee for clients managing multiple AI employees
- **Integration Services** — Bespoke CRM, telephony, and workflow integration work
- **AI Audit & Strategy** — Consulting engagement to identify automation opportunities before deployment

### Unit Economics (Target)
- Average setup fee: $2,500–$10,000 per deployment
- Monthly retainer: $500–$3,000 per AI employee/month
- Target LTV per client: $25,000–$150,000 over 24 months
- Target gross margin: 70%+

### Markets Served
- Primary: United States (English)
- Secondary: Brazil (Portuguese)
- Tertiary: Latin America (Spanish)

## 5. Market Position

Carriersfy AI is not a software company. We are an **AI staffing and operations company**. We compete not against SaaS tools but against the cost and complexity of hiring, training, and retaining human staff for repetitive operational roles.

### Differentiation
- We deploy named, role-specific AI employees — not generic chatbots
- We own the full stack: discovery → build → deploy → maintain
- Bilingual capability (EN/PT/ES) gives us structural advantage in U.S.–Brazil business corridors
- We price against the cost of a human hire, not against software subscriptions

### Competitive Landscape
- Generic AI chatbot vendors (limited scope, no operational integration)
- Traditional automation agencies (slow, expensive, low AI sophistication)
- In-house AI hires (10× the cost, 5× the setup time)

## 6. Founding Principles (Non-Negotiable)

1. **Never ship a broken customer-facing experience.** Every form, every email, every integration must be tested before client delivery.
2. **All client data stays within agreed systems.** No third-party tools unless explicitly approved per client.
3. **Every AI employee must have a defined scope of authority.** No agent acts outside its defined permission boundary.
4. **Cross-link everything.** No document or system is an island. Every decision references its rationale, every task references its project, every client references their active builds.
5. **Version everything.** Every deployment is versioned. Every decision is logged. Every client build has a changelog.
6. **Charge for value, not time.** Our pricing reflects the operational value delivered, not hours spent.
