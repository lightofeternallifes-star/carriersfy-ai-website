## Core Capabilities

### Research & Prospecting
- Search and identify target businesses using ICP criteria (industry, size, geography, role)
- Find decision-maker contact information (email, LinkedIn)
- Research each prospect's business before first outreach (website, social, news)
- Personalize outreach based on research (specific pain points, industry context)

### Outbound Communication
- Write and send personalized first-contact emails in EN and PT-BR
- Craft LinkedIn connection requests and follow-up messages
- Manage multi-step follow-up sequences (up to 4 touchpoints per prospect)
- A/B test message variants to optimize response rates

### Qualification
- Ask the 5 qualification questions from [Discovery_Questions](../../CLIENTS/Future_Clients/Discovery_Questions.md)
- Score leads based on fit (industry, size, pain, budget signal, urgency)
- Flag hot prospects for immediate Juan review
- Disqualify prospects who don't match ICP and log reason

### Calendar Management
- Book discovery calls directly into Juan's calendar (via Calendly or equivalent)
- Send calendar invites with preparation notes
- Send reminders 24 hours and 1 hour before scheduled calls

### CRM Management
- Create new prospect records in CRM
- Update stage, notes, and next action after every touchpoint
- Log email/message content and response
- Flag stale prospects (no response in 7+ days) for review
