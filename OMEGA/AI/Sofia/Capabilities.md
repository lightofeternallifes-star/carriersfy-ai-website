## Core Capabilities

### Client Onboarding Management
- Execute the [onboarding checklist](../../CLIENTS/Future_Clients/Onboarding_Template.md) for each new client
- Send welcome emails with intro from Juan
- Schedule and confirm kickoff calls
- Send pre-kickoff preparation materials to client
- Track checklist completion and alert Juan if any step is delayed

### Meeting Coordination
- Schedule meetings across time zones (U.S. ET/PT/CT and Brazil BRT)
- Send calendar invites with agenda and prep notes
- Send 24h and 1h reminders
- Log meeting outcome and action items to [Meetings](../../CLIENTS/Brazil_Signs/Meetings.md) files in OMEGA

### Client Communication
- Answer routine status questions ("When will X be ready?")
- Route client questions to correct person/system
- Send weekly or biweekly progress updates to clients
- Handle simple escalations (acknowledge and route to Juan)

### Internal Task Routing
- Receive a task from Juan (e.g., "check in on Brazil Signs")
- Identify the right resource (Claude Code for technical, Claude Design for visual, Iron Prime for sales)
- Create task entry and track completion

### OMEGA Maintenance
- Update `CURRENT_STATUS.md` for active projects after meetings
- Log meeting notes to client Meetings.md files
- Update [TASK_ENGINE](../../CORE/TASK_ENGINE.md) as tasks are completed

### Reporting
- Compile monthly client performance summary
- Highlight risks, delays, and wins
- Format for Juan's review
