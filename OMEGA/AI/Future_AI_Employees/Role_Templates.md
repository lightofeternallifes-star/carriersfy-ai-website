# AI Employee Role Templates — Carriersfy AI

> Pre-defined AI employee archetypes for rapid deployment. Last updated: 2026-06-25

---

## Role: Sales Representative AI

**Archetype based on:** Iron Prime  
**Mission:** Identify and warm up prospects until ready for human close  
**Best for:** Companies with a high volume of cold outreach or inbound lead qualification  
**Key capabilities:** Prospect research, personalized outreach, qualification, calendar booking  
**Channels:** Email, LinkedIn, WhatsApp (Brazil)  
**Languages:** EN, PT-BR, ES  
**Deploy time:** 2–3 weeks  
**Monthly value delivered:** Replace 30–40 hrs/week of SDR work  

---

## Role: Operations Coordinator AI

**Archetype based on:** Sofia  
**Mission:** Ensure every internal and client-facing operation runs without human management  
**Best for:** Operations-heavy businesses with scheduling, onboarding, and follow-up needs  
**Key capabilities:** Onboarding management, meeting scheduling, follow-up sequences, status tracking  
**Channels:** Email, WhatsApp, calendar integration  
**Languages:** EN, PT-BR, ES  
**Deploy time:** 2–3 weeks  
**Monthly value delivered:** Replace 20–30 hrs/week of coordinator work  

---

## Role: Customer Support AI

**Mission:** Handle all first-tier customer inquiries and route complex ones to humans  
**Best for:** Companies with high inbound support volume and repetitive FAQs  
**Key capabilities:** FAQ handling, ticket triage, status updates, escalation routing  
**Channels:** Email, web chat, WhatsApp  
**Languages:** EN, PT-BR, ES  
**Deploy time:** 3–4 weeks (needs robust knowledge base)  
**Monthly value delivered:** Reduce support ticket volume by 50–70%  

---

## Role: Appointment Booking AI

**Mission:** Handle all scheduling for service-based businesses  
**Best for:** Clinics, service companies, consultancies — anyone with an appointment-based model  
**Key capabilities:** Calendar access, confirmation emails, reminders, rescheduling  
**Channels:** Web chat, WhatsApp, SMS  
**Languages:** EN, PT-BR, ES  
**Deploy time:** 1–2 weeks  
**Monthly value delivered:** Eliminate 10–20 hrs/week of manual scheduling  

---

## Role: Technical Q&A AI

**Mission:** Answer product/service technical questions for customers or dealers  
**Best for:** Electronics, equipment, SaaS, or any technically complex product  
**Key capabilities:** Product knowledge base Q&A, compatibility checks, troubleshooting, documentation lookup  
**Channels:** Email, web chat, dealer portal  
**Languages:** EN (primarily); PT/ES adaptable  
**Deploy time:** 3–5 weeks (knowledge base setup is the bottleneck)  
**Monthly value delivered:** Replace 20–40 hrs/week of technical support  

---

## Role: Quote Generation AI

**Mission:** Turn inbound quote requests into structured proposals, automatically  
**Best for:** Signage, print, construction, manufacturing — quote-heavy businesses  
**Key capabilities:** Extract requirements from natural language, apply pricing rules, generate quote document  
**Channels:** Email, WhatsApp  
**Languages:** EN, PT-BR  
**Deploy time:** 3–4 weeks (pricing rule definition is the bottleneck)  
**Monthly value delivered:** Cut quote response time from hours to minutes; increase quote volume capacity by 3–5×  

---

## Role: Content & Communication AI

**Mission:** Draft, schedule, and send internal and external communications  
**Best for:** Organizations with regular newsletters, member communications, or social content needs  
**Key capabilities:** Email drafting, newsletter creation, social post drafting, communication calendar  
**Channels:** Email, social media APIs  
**Languages:** EN, PT-BR, ES  
**Deploy time:** 2 weeks  
**Monthly value delivered:** Replace 10–15 hrs/week of content coordination
