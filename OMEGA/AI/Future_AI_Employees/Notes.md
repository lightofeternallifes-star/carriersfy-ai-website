# Notes — Future AI Employees

> Ideas and observations for future agent development. Last updated: 2026-06-25

## Ideas for Future AI Employees

### AI Employee #5: Invoice & Payments AI
- **Use case:** Follow up on unpaid invoices, send payment reminders, reconcile payments
- **Clients:** Any client with net-30/60 terms and cash flow concerns
- **Channels:** Email, WhatsApp
- **Dependency:** Integration with accounting software (QuickBooks, Conta Azul in Brazil, Stripe)
- **Timeline:** Q4 2026

### AI Employee #6: Content Manager AI
- **Use case:** Draft, schedule, and publish social media content and email newsletters
- **Clients:** Faith organizations, retail, any brand with content marketing needs
- **Channels:** Email newsletter, Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook
- **Dependency:** Social media API access, email marketing platform
- **Timeline:** Q4 2026

### AI Employee #7: HR / People Ops AI (Client-Side Deployment)
- **Use case:** Handle employee onboarding, FAQ, PTO tracking, policy questions
- **Clients:** Companies with 20+ employees and repetitive HR questions
- **Channels:** Slack or Teams integration, email
- **Timeline:** 2027

### AI Employee #8: E-Commerce Support AI
- **Use case:** Order status, returns, exchanges, product questions for online stores
- **Clients:** Shopify/WooCommerce stores in Brazil (high WhatsApp support volume)
- **Channels:** WhatsApp, email, web chat
- **Timeline:** 2027

## Platform Vision Note (2026-06-25)

Long-term, every AI employee type above becomes a "product" in the Carriersfy Platform marketplace. Clients browse AI employee roles, select what they need, and we deploy a pre-built, customized version for their business. This reduces deployment time from 3–4 weeks to 3–5 days for standard roles.
