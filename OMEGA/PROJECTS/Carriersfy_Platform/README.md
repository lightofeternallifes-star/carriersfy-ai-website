> Project README | Status: [PLANNED] | Last updated: 2026-06-25

---

## Overview

The Carriersfy Platform is the internal operational backbone of Carriersfy AI — a web application enabling Juan and the AI workforce to manage clients, deploy and monitor AI employees, track workflows, and surface performance data. It will also serve as the client-facing portal where clients can see their deployed AI employees in action.

This project does not yet exist. It is in planning phase as of 2026-06-25.

---

## Why This Exists

As the client base grows beyond 3–5 clients, managing everything manually (or via OMEGA markdown files alone) becomes impractical. The Platform centralizes:
- Client management (contacts, builds, billing)
- AI employee deployment and configuration
- Workflow orchestration
- Performance reporting
- Lead pipeline (when CRM is integrated)
