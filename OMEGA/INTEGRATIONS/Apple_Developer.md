# INTEGRATION: Apple Developer

- **Entity ID:** CF-INT-008
- **Category:** Mobile App Distribution
- **Status:** [PLANNED]
- **Critical:** Low currently

---

## What It Does (Planned)

Apple Developer Program enables distribution of native iOS/macOS applications. Relevant when Carriersfy AI builds a native mobile client for the Carriersfy Platform or AI employee management dashboard.

---

## Current Status

No Apple Developer account has been opened for Carriersfy AI yet. This integration is planned for when:
- Carriersfy Platform (CF-PRJ-002) reaches mobile MVP stage
- A client specifically requests an iOS-native AI employee experience

---

## Prerequisites Before Opening Account

1. Carriersfy Platform (CF-PRJ-002) must be scoped and in development
2. Decision made to build native iOS vs. PWA vs. React Native (new ADR needed)
3. $99/year Apple Developer Program membership fee approved
4. Legal entity verified with Apple (business account vs. individual)

---

## Relationship to Google

A parallel integration for Google Play Store would be needed for Android distribution. Same trigger conditions apply.

---

## Action Required

No action required until CF-PRJ-002 reaches mobile development stage. At that point:
1. Create ADR for mobile strategy (native vs. cross-platform)
2. Open Apple Developer account
3. Update this file with account details
